Indrachandra Campus

Community Campus | Established 2070 B.S

Welcome to Indrachandra Campus

Indrachandra Campus was established in 2070 B.S with the goal of providing quality higher education and academic excellence. The campus has rapidly gained public trust through its academic achievements and dedication to student development. The institution offers an inclusive learning environment with experienced teachers, modern teaching practices, and regular co-curricular activities.

Located in Syaldangi, Jhapa, the campus provides affordable education especially for students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds and remote districts such as Ilam, Panchthar, and Taplejung.

Academic Programs

  • Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS)
  • Master of Business Studies (MBS)

These programs are affiliated with Tribhuvan University and focus on developing strong knowledge in business, finance, administration, and entrepreneurship.

Vision

Quality education for frontier human capital and services.

Mission

To provide quality education using modern technologies and teaching methods, creating creative, entrepreneurial, and socially responsible graduates capable of facing global challenges.

Goals

  • Develop human resources capable of contributing to social and economic development.
  • Promote research-oriented and innovative learning.
  • Provide affordable education to marginalized communities.
