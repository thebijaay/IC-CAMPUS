Programs Offered

Indrachandra Campus offers quality higher education programs affiliated with Tribhuvan University.

Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS)

Management Faculty — Tribhuvan University

The Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS) is a four-year undergraduate program affiliated with Tribhuvan University. The program is structured across four academic years and is designed to equip students with essential knowledge and practical skills in the field of business and management.

The curriculum covers a broad range of subjects including Business English, Microeconomics, Financial Accounting, Business Statistics, Macroeconomics, Business Law, Cost and Management Accounting, Finance, Marketing, Organizational Behavior, and Auditing.

Students develop core competencies in accounting, finance, marketing, and management. The program provides a strong academic foundation that prepares graduates for professional careers in the business sector as well as for higher studies such as MBS or MBA programs.

The BBS program at Indrachandra Campus is known for its experienced faculty, supportive learning environment, and commitment to providing affordable quality education to students from all backgrounds including Dalits, Janajatis, and students from remote hilly districts of eastern Nepal.

Graduates of BBS can pursue careers as accountants, financial analysts, marketing officers, business managers, entrepreneurs, or government officials. The degree also qualifies students to appear in civil service and banking examinations.

Master of Business Studies (MBS)

Management Faculty — Tribhuvan University

The Master of Business Studies (MBS) is a postgraduate program affiliated with Tribhuvan University. The program spans two academic years and is structured across four semesters — First Semester, Second Semester, Third Semester, and Fourth Semester.

The MBS curriculum is designed to build advanced knowledge and skills in the areas of management, finance, marketing, economics, and research methodology. It develops students' analytical thinking, strategic decision-making, and leadership capabilities required in today's competitive business environment.

The program prepares graduates for senior and leadership roles in business organizations, financial institutions, government bodies, non-governmental organizations, and academic institutions. It also provides a solid foundation for students who wish to pursue doctoral studies or independent research in business and management.

At Indrachandra Campus, the MBS program is delivered by highly qualified and experienced faculty who are dedicated to providing students with both theoretical knowledge and practical exposure. The campus maintains a student-friendly environment to help postgraduate students excel in their academic pursuits.

MBS graduates from Indrachandra Campus go on to build successful careers as business executives, financial managers, marketing strategists, research analysts, consultants, academicians, and entrepreneurs across Nepal and beyond.

+2 Management

Higher Secondary — NEB Affiliated

The +2 Management program at Indrachandra Campus is a two-year higher secondary level program affiliated with the National Examinations Board (NEB) of Nepal. It is designed for students who have completed their SEE (Secondary Education Examination) and wish to build a strong foundation in business and management.

The program covers core subjects including Business Studies, Accountancy, Economics, Business Mathematics and Statistics, English, and Social Studies or other optional subjects. These subjects are carefully structured to provide both academic depth and practical relevance.

Students enrolled in the +2 Management stream gain fundamental knowledge of business principles, economic concepts, financial accounting practices, and management theory. This foundation is essential for those planning to pursue undergraduate programs such as BBS, BBA, or other business-related degrees.

Indrachandra Campus is committed to making quality higher secondary education accessible to all students regardless of their socioeconomic background. The campus provides a nurturing academic environment supported by well-qualified teachers who guide students through their formative years of higher education.

Graduates of the +2 Management program are well-prepared to pursue undergraduate studies at Indrachandra Campus itself through the BBS program, or at any other college or university across Nepal. Many alumni have gone on to successful careers in banking, business, and public service after completing this program.
