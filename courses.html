Programs Offered

Indrachandra Campus offers quality higher education programs affiliated with Tribhuvan University.

Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS)

The Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS) is a four-year undergraduate program affiliated with Tribhuvan University. It covers subjects across accounting, finance, marketing, and management, preparing students for professional careers and advanced studies in business.

Master of Business Studies (MBS)

The Master of Business Studies (MBS) is a postgraduate program affiliated with Tribhuvan University, structured across four semesters. It advances students' knowledge in management, research, and leadership, preparing them for senior roles in business and academia.
