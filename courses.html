Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS)

The Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS) is a four-year undergraduate program offered at Indrachandra Campus, Birtamode, Jhapa, affiliated with Tribhuvan University. The program is structured across four academic years and is thoughtfully designed to equip students with essential theoretical knowledge and practical skills in the field of business, management, and commerce. It is one of the most sought-after undergraduate programs at the campus, attracting students from Jhapa, Ilam, Taplejung, Panchthar, Terhathum, Bhojpur, and other districts of eastern Nepal.

The BBS program at Indrachandra Campus has been instrumental in transforming the academic lives of students from all socioeconomic backgrounds, including Dalits, Janajatis, and students from remote hilly communities. The campus has long been committed to making quality higher education accessible and affordable for all. Through dedicated faculty, a supportive academic environment, and a student-centered approach, Indrachandra Campus ensures that every enrolled student receives the guidance they need to excel.

The program provides a comprehensive understanding of core business disciplines including financial accounting, cost and management accounting, business law, economics, marketing, finance, organizational behavior, auditing, and business communication. Students also develop essential soft skills such as critical thinking, analytical reasoning, leadership, and entrepreneurial mindset that are vital for thriving in today's competitive business environment.

For any further queries about the BBS program, students and guardians are encouraged to contact the campus directly at Syaldangi, Birtamode-6, Jhapa. Phone: 023-532799. Email: indrachandracampusbtm@gmail.com. The campus team is always available to guide prospective students through the admission process and program details.

Admission Requirements
  • Candidates must have passed the SEE (Secondary Education Examination) or equivalent from a recognized board.
  • Candidates must have completed the +2 (Higher Secondary) or equivalent program from a recognized institution with at least second division.
  • Candidates from any stream — Science, Management, Humanities, or Education — are eligible to apply for the BBS program.
  • Candidates must fulfill the admission requirements set by Tribhuvan University and Indrachandra Campus for the respective academic year.
Vision of the BBS Program

The BBS program at Indrachandra Campus aims to produce competent, ethical, and socially responsible business graduates who can contribute meaningfully to Nepal's economic development. The program envisions building future business leaders, entrepreneurs, and administrators who are equipped with both academic knowledge and practical business acumen. It is designed to nurture well-rounded graduates who can adapt to the evolving demands of the local and global business landscape while upholding professional integrity and social values.

FAQ
Can I enroll in BBS after completing +2 Science?

Yes. Students from any stream — including Science, Management, Humanities, or Education — who have passed the +2 or equivalent examination from a recognized institution are eligible to apply for the BBS program at Indrachandra Campus.

How many years does the BBS program take to complete?

The BBS program is a four-year undergraduate degree program affiliated with Tribhuvan University. It is structured across four academic years: First Year, Second Year, Third Year, and Fourth Year.

What career opportunities are available after BBS?

BBS graduates can pursue careers as accountants, financial analysts, marketing officers, bank officers, business managers, entrepreneurs, or government officials. The degree also qualifies graduates to appear in civil service, banking, and other competitive examinations in Nepal.

Can I pursue MBS after completing BBS from Indrachandra Campus?

Yes. After successfully completing the BBS program, graduates are eligible to apply for the Master of Business Studies (MBS) program, which is also offered at Indrachandra Campus and is affiliated with Tribhuvan University.

Is scholarship available for the BBS program?

Indrachandra Campus is committed to inclusive education and provides scholarship opportunities for students from marginalized communities, Dalits, Janajatis, and economically disadvantaged backgrounds. Please contact the campus administration for details about available scholarships and eligibility criteria.

BBS Curriculum Overview

The BBS curriculum is structured across four academic years under Tribhuvan University. Each year introduces students to progressively advanced concepts in business, management, accounting, economics, finance, and related disciplines. The syllabus is designed to provide both theoretical foundations and practical business applications.

  • Year 1: Foundation of Business Studies

    The first year introduces students to the fundamental concepts of business and management. Core subjects include Business English, Principles of Management, Business Economics (Micro), Financial Accounting, and Business Mathematics and Statistics.

    • Year 1 — Subject 1: Business English and Communication

      This subject focuses on developing students' written and verbal communication skills essential for professional business environments. Students learn report writing, correspondence, and presentation techniques.

    • Year 1 — Subject 2: Financial Accounting

      Students are introduced to the principles and practices of financial accounting including journal entries, ledger posting, trial balance, and preparation of final accounts for sole traders and partnerships.

  • Year 2: Intermediate Business and Accounting

    The second year builds upon the foundational concepts introduced in the first year. Students study Macroeconomics, Corporate Accounting, Business Law, Cost and Management Accounting, and Marketing.

    • Year 2 — Subject 1: Cost and Management Accounting

      This subject equips students with the tools to analyze costs, prepare budgets, and use management accounting information for business decision-making and performance evaluation.

    • Year 2 — Subject 2: Business Law

      Students gain a comprehensive understanding of Nepal's legal framework governing business activities, including contract law, company law, negotiable instruments, and consumer protection regulations.

  • Year 3: Advanced Management and Finance

    In the third year, students advance their knowledge in key areas including Financial Management, Organizational Behavior and Human Resource Management, Income Tax and VAT, Auditing, and Fundamentals of E-Business and Information Technology.

  • Year 4: Strategic Business and Research

    The final year focuses on strategic and applied business knowledge. Students study Strategic Management, International Business, Entrepreneurship, Project Work, and optional specialization subjects in Finance, Marketing, or Accountancy. The year also includes a research project to develop independent analytical skills.
