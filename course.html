Programs Offered

Indrachandra Campus offers quality higher education programs affiliated with Tribhuvan University.

Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS)

The Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS) program focuses on developing students’ managerial and business skills. It prepares students for careers in accounting, finance, marketing, and management while also providing a foundation for higher studies.

Master of Business Studies (MBS)

The Master of Business Studies (MBS) program is designed to enhance students' analytical, managerial, and leadership skills. It prepares graduates for advanced roles in business organizations, research, and entrepreneurship.

+2 Management

The +2 Management program provides students with fundamental knowledge of business, economics, accounting, and management. It builds a strong academic foundation for students who wish to pursue business and management studies in higher education.
