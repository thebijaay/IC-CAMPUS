Welcome to Indrachandra Campus

Indrachandra Campus was established in 2070 B.S with the aim of providing quality education by achieving academic excellence. Within a short period of time, the campus has grown rapidly and gained public trust through its strong academic performance.

Situated in an urban area, the campus serves students from low economic backgrounds. The increasing number of students admitted to the campus reflects its positive reputation and commitment to providing accessible education.

The campus offers a peaceful environment with clean surroundings, dedicated teachers, its own building, and regular co-curricular activities. These features contribute to the overall development of students both academically and socially.

Indrachandra Campus is one of the emerging community campuses. It has been established purely for academic development rather than profit making. The campus provides educational opportunities at affordable fees so that students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds can pursue higher education.

The campus has provided educational opportunities to people from remote and rural areas of the northern part of Jhapa district. Dalits, Janajatis, married women, minorities, and other marginalized groups have greatly benefited from the educational facilities offered by the campus.

Students from nearby eastern hill districts such as Ilam, Panchthar, and Taplejung are also benefiting from the academic programs provided by the campus.

Due to transparency, dedication, and strong academic results, the campus has gained significant public support and continues to compete successfully with other educational institutions.
